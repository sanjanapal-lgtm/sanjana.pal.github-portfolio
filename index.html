Featured Project
UCD Smurfit · 2026
Competitive Strategy & Market Positioning

RSM Ireland

As part of a five-member student consulting team engaged through UCD Smurfit's Diagnostic Techniques in Consulting module, I worked directly with RSM Ireland — one of Ireland's top ten accounting and advisory firms and a member of RSM International, the 6th largest assurance, tax and consulting network globally.

The engagement was structured as a live client project, with RSM Ireland as the sponsoring organisation. Our team was tasked with delivering a comprehensive competitive analysis and go-to-market strategy review to help RSM Ireland strengthen its brand positioning in the Irish professional services market.

"This project sharpened my ability to translate market intelligence into actionable consulting recommendations under real client conditions — bridging structured analysis with practical, people-first strategy."
What We Did
  • 01
    Competitor Benchmarking
    Evaluated RSM Ireland against Grant Thornton, BDO, Mazars, and the Big Four (Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG) across brand visibility, service innovation, and sector specialisation.
  • 02
    Brand Awareness Assessment
    Assessed RSM Ireland's current market recognition relative to competitors and identified perception gaps among target client segments.
  • 03
    Addressable Market Analysis
    Mapped the professional services landscape in Ireland across audit, tax, and consulting, and estimated realistic growth segments aligned with RSM's capabilities.
  • 04
    Strategic Recommendations
    Developed actionable, resource-conscious recommendations to enhance RSM Ireland's share of voice, differentiation, and client engagement strategy.
